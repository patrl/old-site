I'm a PhD candidate in the Linguistics Department at University College London funded by the Arts and Humanities Research Council. I mainly work on the syntax-semantics interface, but I'm also interested in semantics proper, morphosyntax, pragmatics, and experimental linguistics. My research page is updated regularly with papers and handouts. I'm currently a member of the LAGB Student Committee, so get in touch if you're a grad student looking to fund a student conference or workshop.

Along with Ezekiel Panitz, I'm organizing a workshop on the syntax and semantics of interrogatives, to take place at UCL in September. Check out the workshop page for more information.
